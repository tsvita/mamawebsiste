About Me

I am a passionate, creative, self taught chef with a background in studio art which means my food is both delicious and visually appealing. When I have free time you can find me happily experimenting in the kitchen – creating and refining recipes, attempting new techniques, exploring unfamiliar cuisines.

There are few things I enjoy more than eating good food with friends and family. I am delighted to provide food for your social gatherings so you can spend time with your special people and not feel stressed about food preparation. I have been a kosher caterer for thirty plus years.
